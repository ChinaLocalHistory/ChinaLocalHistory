D. Head Gate/Victory Report Gate, Jiebao Men 捷報門
Head Gate was built at the same time as Seriousness and Consonance Hall was built (1456-1462). It was renovated in 1825 and again in 1988.
E. Ceremonial Gate/Glory of the Nation Gate, Guoguang Men 國光門
The Ceremonial Gate was built at the same time as Seriousness and Consonance Hall, in the mid-fifteenth century. It was renovated in 1867 and 1985. It is said that when officials of the third rank or above visited the compound the middle gate would be opened and the threshold removed in order to show respect.
F. Seriousness and Consonance Hall, Suyong Hall, Suyong Tang 肅雍堂
Account of Seriousness (su) and Consonance (yong) Hall
Suyong tang, Seriousness and Consonance Hall, was built between 1456 and 1462 by Lu Rong. It is the very center of the compound. The name `seriousness and consonance` (suyong) comes from the Classic of Poetry, one of the Confucian Classics. It is the most important public space in the compound and the location for lineage-wide rituals, celebrations, entertainment, and discussions.

K. Virtue Through the Generations Gateway, Shide Menfang 世德門坊
The Virtue through the Generations Gateway was built in the first half of the 17th century. It was destroyed in the 1950s. The gateway we see today was moved from Liutang Village in 1993.

O. Good Blessings Hall, Shanqing Tang 善慶堂
Good Blessings Hall was built during 1851-1861. Originally it was located behind the Lu Lineage Ancestral Hall. It was moved to its current location in 1993.
P. Pleasant Gatherings Hall, Dunxu Tang 惇敘堂
Pleasant Gatherings Hall was built in the later half of the fifteenth century by Lu Ben. It was originally located elsewhere and was moved to the current location after the flood in 1989. The rooms on the two sides were built during the renovation.
